3. How to respond

Respond online

You can respond online if:

  • your husband or wife applied using the online divorce service
  • you've received a letter or email from the court with your reference number and access code

You should read the rest of this guide before you respond.

Respond by post

If your husband or wife has started divorce proceedings against you using the paper forms, the divorce centre will send you a copy of the divorce application in the post.

You will also get:

  • a notice of proceedings letter
  • an acknowledgment of service form

Keep the notice of proceedings, which tells you the case number and what you should do next.

Fill in the acknowledgement of service form. You must send this to the court by the deadline.

You should read the rest of this guide before you respond.
