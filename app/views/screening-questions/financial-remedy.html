Before you begin you should consider how you'll divide your money and property when you divorce. You can make the division legally binding with a 'financial order'.

You need to get a financial order if you:

  • have nothing to split but want to make your financial separation final
  • agree on how you want to share your money and property and want to make that legally binding
  • disagree on the split and want the court to settle the dispute

You should read more about financial orders before you continue as you'll be asked if you want to apply for one later in this application.
