Before you begin

This is a new service to complete the divorce application online. The court will send you the rest of the forms to complete the divorce by email. We’re working to bring the rest of the process online.

Fees

You'll need to pay the £550 fee by debit or credit card at the end of this application. That's usually all you'll have to pay.

However, you may have to pay more later on if:

  • your husband or wife tries to prevent the divorce (known as defending it)
  • the divorce papers can't be delivered to them
  • you have to change your application

You can get help paying these fees if you have little or no savings, are on certain benefits or have a low income.

Have you settled your finances?

Before you begin you should consider how you'll divide your money and property. You can make the division legally binding if you get a 'financial order'.

You need to get a financial order if you:

  • have nothing to split but want to make your financial separation final
  • agree on how you want to share your money and property and want to make that legally binding
  • disagree on how to split your money and property and want the court to settle the dispute
