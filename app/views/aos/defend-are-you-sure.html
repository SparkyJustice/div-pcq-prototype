If you intend to prevent the divorce

You'll need to submit another form (known as the 'answer') with your reason for defending the divorce. You'll also have to pay another £245 fee.

You have 21 days to submit your answer from the date that you first signed into your divorce account.

If you don't submit the answer by the deadline, your {{ respsHusbandOrWife() }} will usually be able to continue with the divorce as if you didn't defend it.

Making your own application for divorce

You'll need to complete a new divorce application and pay the £550 fee if you want to apply for the divorce yourself. You have 21 days to submit your answer from the date that you first signed into your divorce account.
