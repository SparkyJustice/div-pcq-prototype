Your ‘answer’ to the divorce application has been received by the court.

A Judge will now decide whether you and your wife will need to attend a hearing. You may be contacted for more information to help them make that decision.

You’ll receive a letter in the post telling you if you need to attend a hearing, and when and where that hearing is.
