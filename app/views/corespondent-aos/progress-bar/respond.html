Read the divorce application

You need to read the divorce application before you respond. You can find a copy in the letter that was sent to you, and a digital version with guidance on what each section means on the next screen.

Your response is legally known as the 'acknowledgement of service'.

You must respond to the application within 7 days. If you don’t, the divorce may move forwards without you.

As you have been named as a co-respondent in the divorce, the applicant is allowed to claim the costs of the divorce from you. This could include the £550 application fee, and their legal fees and expenses. If you don't respond, you won't have a say in whether or not you pay these costs.
