You need to submit your answer

To defend the divorce you need to submit the 'answer to a divorce application' form. The deadline for this is 21 days from the date that you first signed in to respond to the application.

Download and fill in the paper form.

Post the form to:

  • East Midlands Regional Divorce Centre
  • PO Box 10447
  • Nottingham
  • NG2 9QN

You'll have to pay a £245 fee when you submit the form.

You'll probably have to go to a hearing with your {{ respsHusbandOrWife() }} to try to come to an agreement over the divorce.

If you don't submit your answer before the deadline, then your {{ respsHusbandOrWife() }} can continue the divorce as if you hadn't said that you intended to defend the divorce.
