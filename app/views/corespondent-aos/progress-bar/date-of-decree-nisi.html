The application for a decree nisi has been accepted

A district judge will formally announce the decree nisi at a court hearing. This is the first stage of the decree ending the marriage.

The court has also decided to make an order that you pay towards the divorce costs.

The divorce is only complete when the decree absolute has been granted.

The hearing

You can find more details about the date of the hearing and the orders that will be made (including costs orders) in the Entitlement to a decree (PDF, 69kb).

Warning You don’t need to come to the hearing unless you want to object to the costs order.

If you want to attend the hearing, you need to email the court before the hearing date. If you want to dispute a costs order, the court must receive a letter or email stating the reasons for doing so at least 14 days before the hearing date.
