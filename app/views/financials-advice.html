When you should get a financial order by

You should get a financial order before your divorce is finalised otherwise your legal rights will change. This will affect what you’re entitled to. For example, you will have to pay any capital gains tax on assets that are transferred between you.

You should get legal advice if you're not sure what to do.

When you're ready to complete your financial order application

You'll need to complete another form and pay another fee to formally start financial proceedings. How much you'll need to pay will depend on what type of order you get. The court will only start processing your request for a financial order when you've done this.

You’ll be given a link to more guidance when you finish your divorce application.
