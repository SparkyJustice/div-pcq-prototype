Do you want to apply for a financial order?

If you can't agree how your assets will be split you can ask the court to make a financial order. This can be used to decide how your money and property are divided and to order your wife to pay you maintenance. A financial order is the only way to make the split legally binding. You can apply for orders for yourself, and your children if you have any.
