What does habitually resident and domiciled mean?

Habitually resident means your life is mainly based in England and Wales, you’re settled here and you intend to remain settled here. You may work here, own property, have your children in school here, and your main family life may take place here.

Domiciled means that you consider England and Wales to be your permanent home. You can be domiciled and live abroad as long as you intend to return here.

If you don't think this applies to you, you can choose how you're connected to England and Wales.
