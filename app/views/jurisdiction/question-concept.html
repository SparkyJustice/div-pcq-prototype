'Habitual residence' means that you consider England and Wales to be your home. You're settled here and spend most of your life here either in work, education, or with your family.

If you both have 'habitual residence' in England and Wales you can use the courts here to get a divorce.

There are other ways of falling within the courts jurisdiction however. You can strengten your case for jurisdiction if you want to and present more connections to England and Wales.
