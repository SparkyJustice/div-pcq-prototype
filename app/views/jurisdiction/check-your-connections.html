What is habitual residence?

What does domiciled mean?

You consider England and Wales to be your permanent home, and have one of the following connections:

  • your father was domiciled here when you were born (if your parent's were married)
  • your mother was domiciled here when you were born (if your parent's weren't married)
  • you've settled in England and Wales and intend to stay here for the rest of your life, for example by becoming a citizen

This isn’t a complete list of everything that indicates domicile. If you’re not sure, you should get legal advice.

You can be domiciled and live abroad as long as you intend to return here.
