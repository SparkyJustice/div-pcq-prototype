Help with habitual residence and domicile

Habitual residence

Your habitual residence is the place in which your life is mainly based. You must be settled there and intend to stay settled there. For example, you work there, own property, have your children in school there, and your main family life takes place there.

Domicile

Your domicile is the main permanent home in which you live, or to which you intend to return.
