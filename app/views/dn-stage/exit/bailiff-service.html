If you think that your {{ petsHusbandOrWife() }} is deliberately not responding to the divorce application you can pay for a court bailiff to deliver it to them.

If the bailiff succeeds in delivering the application to your {{ petsHusbandOrWife() }}, you'll be given a 'certificate of service' which you can then send to the court. This will mean that you can continue with your divorce without your {{ petsHusbandOrWife() }}'s response.

If the bailiff can't deliver the application (eg because they can't find them) you'll need to either:

  • use another method of delivering the application
  • apply to the court to dispense with (not need) a response
