{% if session.facts.reason == "adultery" %}

You can’t use an act of adultery as a reason for divorce if you lived together as a couple for more than 6 months after you found out about it.

This could have been in one go, or if you lived together on and off for several periods of time adding up to more than 6 months.

{% endif %} {% if session.facts.reason == "unreasonable-behaviour" %}

If you lived together as a couple for more than 6 months after the behaviour occured, the court may take this into account in its decision on whether to grant you a divorce.

This is because it may be an indication that the marriage hasn't in fact broken down irretrievably.

This could have been in one go, or if you've lived together on and off for several periods adding up to more than 6 months.

{% endif %} {% if session.facts.reason == "separation-2-years" %}

You must have lived apart from your {{ petsHusbandOrWife() }} for at least 2 years since you separated.

Any time that you've spent living together since you separated doesn't count towards your separation period.

The court will add up any time that you've lived together since you separated. The combined total could affect the date that you can apply using this reason for divorce.

Living together for more than 6 months

If you've lived together for a total of more than 6 months since you separated, then you can't rely on that period of separation as the basis for your 2 year separation period.

If this is the case for you, you'll have to use the next date that you separated as a starting point for your 2 years separation period.

Living together for less than 6 months

If you've lived together for a total of less than 6 months since you separated, you can still use this date. However, you'll need to make up any time during which you were living together.

For example if you separated on March 2014, lived together from May 2014 to July 2014 (3 months), then lived apart from Aug 2014, the earliest you could apply for divorce based on your original separation date would be June 2016 (2 years and 3 months from March 2014).

{% endif %} {% if session.facts.reason == "separation-5-years" %}
